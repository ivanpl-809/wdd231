Site Plan: Local Restaurant Website

Site Name

Site Name: The Rustic Spoon

Reason: The name reflects the cozy, rustic vibe of the restaurant, which specializes in homemade, comfort food. It evokes warmth and simplicity, aligning with the restaurant's brand identity.

Optional Domain: therusticspoon.com (available for purchase)

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a digital hub for The Rustic Spoon, providing visitors with information about the restaurant's menu, location, business hours, and reservation options. It will also showcase the restaurant's ambiance and specialties through high-quality images and engaging content, encouraging visitors to dine in or make reservations.

Scenarios

  • What are the restaurant's hours of operation, and are they open on holidays?
  • Does the restaurant offer vegetarian or gluten-free options?
  • How can I make a reservation for a special occasion?
  • Where is the restaurant located, and is there parking available?

Color Schema

The color scheme reflects the warm, rustic, and inviting atmosphere of the restaurant:

  • Primary Color: #8B4513 - Used for headings, accents, and buttons.
  • Secondary Color: #F4A460 - Used for backgrounds and highlights.
  • Accent Color: #FFFFFF - Used for text and contrast elements.
  • Background Color: #FFF8DC - Used for the main background to keep the design light and clean.

Typography

The typography will enhance the restaurant's rustic and welcoming theme:

  • Primary Font: "Playfair Display" (serif)
  • Secondary Font: "Open Sans" (sans-serif)

Wireframe

Below are the wireframe sketches for the home page layout:

Mobile View

Layout:

  • Header with restaurant name and navigation menu (collapsible for mobile).
  • Hero section with a large image and tagline.
  • Introduction section with a brief description of the restaurant.
  • Links to other pages (Menu, Location, Reservations).
  • Footer with social media links and attributions.

Desktop View

Layout:

  • Header with restaurant name and horizontal navigation menu.
  • Hero section with a full-width image and tagline.
  • Introduction section with a two-column layout (text on one side, image on the other).
  • Links to other pages displayed as cards or buttons.
  • Footer with social media links, contact info, and attributions.
